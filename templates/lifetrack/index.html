LifeTrack

Your routine, your rules

Welcome to LifeTrack, the ultimate tool for taking control of your daily habits and routines. At LifeTrack, we believe that your routine should work for you, not the other way round. That's why our platform is designed to help youh effortlessly track, manage, and optimize your habits, so you can live life on your own terms. Whether you're focusing on fitness, productivity, mindfulness, or personal growth, LifeTrack adapts to your unique lifestyle and keeps you motivated every step of the way.

About us

We (four passionate computer science students — Kseniia, Aman, Nico, and Tanishaa) created LifeTrack to make habit tracking simple, effective, and accessible for everyone. Inspired by our own struggles to stay consistent with routines, we developed LifeTrack to help people take control of their daily habits and achieve their personal goals. With a shared vision of empowering individuals to live intentionally, our team combined technical skills and creativity to build a platform that’s both user-friendly and powerful. LifeTrack is more than just a tool, it’s a reflection of our commitment to helping others create meaningful, lasting change in their lives. Join us on this journey, and let LifeTrack help you turn your routines into rules that work for you!
